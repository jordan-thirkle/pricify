# Pricify

> **Stop guessing what to charge. Start knowing.**

Four free SaaS pricing tools in one dashboard. Turn your costs, customers, and growth goals into a pricing plan that works — built by an indie maker for indie makers.

## Features

### 💲 Pricing Calculator
Enter your monthly costs, desired margin, and customer count to calculate the ideal price point. Adjust variables and see results instantly.

### ∞ Freemium Calculator
Model your freemium conversion funnel. Set free-tier limits, conversion rates, and premium pricing to find the sweet spot that maximizes revenue.

### ↗ SaaS Metrics Dashboard
Track and visualize key SaaS metrics — MRR, churn, LTV, CAC, and more. Understand your unit economics at a glance.

### ◎ Stripe Payment Link Generator
Generate Stripe payment links directly from the app. Configure prices, subscription intervals, and metadata without leaving your browser.

### Why Pricify?

- **Free forever** — No account, no signup, no tracking
- **Instant feedback** — Every change updates immediately
- **Indie-maker focused** — Built by someone who got tired of spreadsheets
- **Ship-ready** — Copy Stripe links and launch with confidence

## How It Works

1. **Enter Your Numbers** — Monthly costs, customer count, growth goals. Whatever you know, plug it in.
2. **See the Results** — Every change updates instantly. Play with conversion rates, churn, and pricing until it feels right.
3. **Ship With Confidence** — Copy the Stripe code. Set up your payment page. Launch knowing your unit economics work.
